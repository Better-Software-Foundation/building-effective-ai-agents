# Milestone A: Synthesis Foundation

## Objective

Establish the shared content and attribution model required to expand the app from an Anthropic adaptation into a synthesized interactive guide covering Anthropic and OpenAI.

## Product decisions

- Anthropic remains the stronger source for architecture patterns, business framing, and evolution strategy.
- OpenAI becomes the stronger source for agent foundations, orchestration mechanics, and guardrails.
- The app must preserve source-level attribution at the section and concept level.
- The product should synthesize both sources without flattening meaningful terminology differences.

## Canonical route architecture

- `/summary` Executive Summary
- `/patterns` Architecture Patterns
- `/decision` Decision Framework
- `/foundations` Foundations & Guardrails
- `/compare` Comparative Guide

## Milestone A deliverables

1. Shared source metadata model
2. Shared route architecture model
3. Terminology crosswalk dataset
4. Source badge component
5. Placeholder routes for future synthesized experiences

## Planned follow-on milestones

- Milestone B: build Foundations & Guardrails and the agent/no-agent gate
- Milestone C: upgrade patterns with source mapping
- Milestone D: comparative guide and breadth-vs-depth matrix
- Milestone E: homepage and narrative polish
